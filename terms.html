Exam Retake Support

Professional and Career Accelerator students who purchase their voucher through us have their second attempt covered if they don't pass. Professional and Career Accelerator students who fail both attempts while meeting documented eligibility requirements (80% live session attendance, completed practice exams, two score reports submitted) may request a seat in a future cohort at no additional cost. Vouchers for additional attempts are not included.

To be eligible for a complimentary future seat, students must have:

  • Attended at least 80% of live sessions
  • Completed all assigned practice exams during the program
  • Provided proof of two exam attempts via score reports submitted to info@voyagercyber.com

Note: Exam retake support provides a seat in a future cohort only—it does not include the purchase of additional exam vouchers beyond the initial bundle provided at enrollment.
