Refund Policy

Our refund, transfer, and retake support policies — written plainly so there are no surprises.

Last updated: April 2026

Requested more than 14 days before cohort start

🔄

❌

No Refund or Transfer

After the cohort has begun

Refunds

Full refunds are available for any reason if requested more than 14 days before the cohort start date. To request a refund, email info@voyagercyber.com with your name and enrollment details. Refunds will be processed within 10 business days to the original payment method.

Transfers

If you are within 14 days of the cohort start date and are unable to attend, you may request a one-time transfer of your enrollment to a future cohort. Transfers are not available after the cohort has begun and may only be used once per enrollment. To request a transfer, email info@voyagercyber.com before the first session.

After the Cohort Begins

Once the cohort has started, no refunds or transfers will be issued regardless of the reason for withdrawal, including personal circumstances, scheduling conflicts, or dissatisfaction with the program. We encourage you to reach out to us before the cohort begins if you have any concerns — we would rather find a solution than lose a student.

Retake Support

We stand behind this program. Students who complete the bootcamp and do not pass the CompTIA Security+ exam after two attempts may request one complimentary seat in a future cohort.

To be eligible for retake support, students must have:

  • Attended at least 80% of live sessions, or watched the equivalent via session recordings
  • Completed all assigned practice exams during the program
  • Provided proof of two exam attempts (score reports submitted to info@voyagercyber.com)

Retake support provides a seat in a future cohort only — it does not include an additional exam voucher. Students who require a second voucher may purchase one separately. Retake support is available to students who purchased any enrollment tier and is not dependent on having purchased a voucher through Voyager Cyber.

Cohort Cancelled by Voyager Cyber

If Voyager Cyber cancels a cohort for any reason, enrolled students will receive a full refund of all amounts paid within 10 business days, or the option to transfer to the next available cohort at no additional cost.

Waitlist

Joining the waitlist is free and non-binding. No payment is collected at the waitlist stage, and you are under no obligation to enroll.

Questions

If you have any questions about this policy before enrolling, we're happy to answer them. Reach us at info@voyagercyber.com
