Privacy Policy

How Voyager Cyber collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: April 2026

What We Collect

When you submit our waitlist or enrollment form, we collect the following information:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your tier of interest (Starter, Professional, or Career Accelerator)
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide in the form

How We Use It

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Contact you about enrollment status, cohort dates, and program updates
  • Answer questions you send us directly
  • Send occasional updates about Voyager Cyber programs (you can opt out at any time by emailing info@voyagercyber.com)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

How Your Data Is Processed

Our waitlist and enrollment forms are built on Google Forms, which is operated by Google LLC. Submissions are stored in Google's infrastructure and subject to Google's Privacy Policy. By submitting a form on our site, you acknowledge that your data is processed through Google's systems.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to communicate with you about the program. If you are not enrolled and have not heard from us in 12 months, your information will be deleted from our active records.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@voyagercyber.com. We will respond within 10 business days.

Cookies

Our website does not directly use tracking cookies. However, third-party services embedded in our site (such as Google Forms) may set their own cookies in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our business grows. Any significant changes will be noted with an updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
