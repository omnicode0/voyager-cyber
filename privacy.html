Privacy Policy

How Voyager Cyber collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: June 2026

What We Collect

When you interact with our site, book training, or enroll, we collect:

  • Personal details: Your name, email address, and tier of interest.
  • Booking & Payment data: Information needed to schedule training and process payments securely (we do not store full credit card numbers on our servers).
  • Usage & Analytics data: Information about how you navigate our site, your IP address, browser type, and interaction data (such as clicks and scrolling).

How We Use It

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Contact you about enrollment status, cohort dates, and program updates
  • Answer questions you send us directly
  • Send occasional updates about Voyager Cyber programs (you can opt out at any time by emailing info@voyagercyber.com)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

How Your Data Is Processed (Third-Party Services)

We use trusted third-party tools to run our academy securely and efficiently. By using our site, you acknowledge that your data may be processed by the following services according to their respective privacy policies:

  • Analytics & Behavior: We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and Microsoft Clarity to understand website traffic and see how users interact with our pages. Clarity captures behavioral metrics (like heatmaps and session replays) but automatically masks sensitive keystrokes to protect your privacy.
  • Payments: All transactions are processed securely through Stripe. Your payment details are handled directly by Stripe's encrypted infrastructure.
  • Communications: We use Brevo to manage our email communications, program updates, and waitlists.
  • Scheduling & Intake: We use Google Calendar to manage cohort schedules and Calendly for admissions consultations.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to communicate with you about the program. If you are not enrolled and have not heard from us in 12 months, your information will be deleted from our active records.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@voyagercyber.com. We will respond within 10 business days.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to function properly and help us analyze site traffic. Specifically, Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity place tracking cookies on your browser to measure site performance and user interaction. Third-party tools like Stripe and Google Calendar may also set cookies to facilitate secure payments and bookings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our business grows. Any significant changes will be noted with an updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of our site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
