Why I Created Voyager Cyber Academy

Many cybersecurity students struggle because most training is unengaging, entirely self-paced, or disconnected from the real world.

I created Voyager Cyber Academy to provide structured, live instruction that helps beginners and career changers build practical understanding, confidence, and momentum toward cybersecurity careers.

Drawing from experience in cyber operations, technical education, and systems security, this program focuses on helping students deeply understand the material so they can pass Security+ and continue growing in the field long after the certification exam.
